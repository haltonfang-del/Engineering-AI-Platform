# Project Vision

## Vision

Engineering AI Platform aims to become an enterprise-grade AI platform that assists engineering teams throughout the entire product development lifecycle.

The platform integrates engineering knowledge, AI assistants, workflow automation, and project documentation into a unified ecosystem.

---

# Mission

Our mission is to:

- Improve engineering efficiency.
- Preserve engineering knowledge.
- Reduce repetitive work.
- Standardize engineering workflows.
- Enable AI-assisted engineering collaboration.

---

# Target Users

Primary users include:

- Mechanical Engineers
- Electrical Engineers
- Manufacturing Engineers
- Project Managers
- Engineering Managers

Future versions may support additional engineering disciplines.

---

# Core Values

## Documentation First

Documentation is treated as part of the product.

Every major decision should be documented before implementation.

---

## AI Collaboration

AI is a development partner rather than a replacement for engineers.

The platform is designed for collaboration between engineers and multiple AI assistants.

---

## Single Source of Truth

GitHub Repository is the authoritative source for:

- Documentation
- Architecture
- AI Context
- Source Code
- Project Status

---

## Extensibility

Every component should be designed for future expansion.

Avoid tightly coupled implementations.

---

# Long-Term Objectives

The long-term vision includes:

- AI Knowledge Management
- Engineering Copilot
- Prompt Management
- Multi-AI Routing
- Engineering Document Search
- Design Review Assistant
- Engineering Workflow Automation
- Internal Engineering Chat Platform

---

# Non-Goals

The project does not aim to:

- Replace engineering judgement.
- Automatically generate production-ready designs without review.
- Become a generic chatbot.
- Lock users into a single AI provider.

---

# Success Criteria

The platform is considered successful when it can:

- Centralize engineering knowledge.
- Support multiple AI providers.
- Provide consistent engineering guidance.
- Improve engineering productivity.
- Reduce repetitive engineering tasks.
